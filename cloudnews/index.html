CloudNews

Feed reader for Nextcloud

CloudNews is a feed (RSS and Atom) reader for iOS and macOS. CloudNews syncs with the News app on your NextCloud server (server access is required).

  • Both iOS and macOS fully supported with native apps.
  • Feeds can be added by entering their address (url) and will be synced.
  • You can choose to read just the summary of an article or the original web version.
  • Articles can be shared using several services.
  • Organize feeds in folders
